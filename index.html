Simple piskel

Description

Simple piskel is a simple version of Piskel application, which was created as a study project.

Simple piskel is a free online editor for animated sprites & pixel art. Create animations in your browser and then export result as .apng or .gif file to your system.

Example sprites

Functionality overview

  • You can choose one of three canvas sizes (32*32), (64*64), (128*128);
  • There are available different tools, such as pen, bucket, eraser, paint all pixels of the same color;
  • You can choose one of four pen sizes;
  • Check a preview of your animation in real time as you draw. Adjust the frame delay on the fly;
